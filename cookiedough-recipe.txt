Ingredients: 
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup flour
1 tblspoon vanilla extract
1/2 tspoon salt
1 stick softened butter
1/2 cup chocolate chips
2 tblspoons milk

Instructions

1. Combine butter and brown sugar. Mix thoroughly.

2. Combine vanilla extract and salt, and mix them into the batter.

3. Slowly mix in the flour.

4. Add the milk, and mix it in. It should resemble typical cookie dough now.

5. Mix in the chocolate chips.

6. Refrigerate.
